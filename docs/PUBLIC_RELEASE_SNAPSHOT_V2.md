## Main scientific statement of v2

The Block 4 material supports a stronger public claim than before:

> an isolated real note should not be interpreted as one clean tone followed by
> meaningless noisy residue.
> It is better interpreted as a causal resonance system consisting of harmonic
> cores, generated resonance chains, note-box growth, residual continuation,
> body response, and still-unresolved but meaningful distant responses.

This applies even under controlled isolated-note recording conditions.

## Why this matters publicly

This snapshot gives outside researchers a compact way to verify that:

- the pipeline is runnable;
- the model produces structured outputs;
- the results are not only aesthetic visualizations;
- the interpretation of `unassigned resonance` is principled rather than dismissive.
